Addison Kline is the founder Baseball Analytica as well as the site's lead developer and writer. Alongside writing posts, Kline has created and managed multiple site tools, including Baseball Analytica's ranking system and FOXY projection model. His primary baseball interests include team performance, analyzing existing metrics, and creating new ones. Kline is an undergraduate at Washington University in St. Louis studying mathematics and computer science.
